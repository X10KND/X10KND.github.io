Random Card Number Generator


This web application is designed to streamline the process of distributing exam cards randomly.

How to Use:
*** Open the index.html File: Simply double-click the index.html file to launch the application in your web browser.
*** Give Minimum and Maximum card numbers.
*** Generate Card Numbers: Click the "Generate" button to obtain a set of random card numbers.
*** You can reset the button after each session

P.S.
The browser based app consist of all the three files.
Do not delete any of the three files. Keep these in the same folder.
